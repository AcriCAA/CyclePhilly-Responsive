Cycle Philly

Cycle Philly is a smartphone app for recording your bicycle trips. Data from the app can be used by transportation planners in the City of Philadelphia to make Philly a better place to ride.

About

Cycle Philly was developed by Code for Philly, an open group of citizens working to harness the power of technology to modernize citizenship in Philadelphia.

We hope that data collected by the app will be used by planners in the City of Philadelphia and Delaware Valley to build better bike routes.
